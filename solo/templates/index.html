About SOLO

Here at SOLO we are looking to help you find your perfect on campus roomate. SOLO brings you the tools to help make the process of finding a roomate a whole lot easier for you. SOLO can be used to help you talk to other students, show you all the building options on campus, and match you directly with other people who have the same interests as you. SOLO makes sure you aren't riding solo into your UNCC experiece.

About UNCC

About The SOLO Team

We are all UNCC students of the 2019 class. We decicded to create SOLO as an idea for our software development project. We experienced the process of rooming and had some unfortunate roomates ourselves. So it became our objective to help others with this struggle by making their experience easier and more enjoyable than what we had to deal with. We hope this website brings all of you closer to finding the perfect roommate!
